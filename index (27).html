Why We Are Here

Cold Shirts. Warm Hearts.

We built this collection piece by piece, hunting directly through local thrift stores right in Juneau, Alaska. Every faded graphic, old-school logo, and worn hem holds a real piece of history sourced straight from the racks of a single southeast coastal town.

But these shirts didn't travel thousands of miles just to look good.

Every single dollar raised from this collection goes directly toward supporting J.R.'s medical recovery and rehabilitation journey. We're proud to bring a piece of the Far North to Victoria, Texas, rally local support, and back J.R. every step of the way.

The Curated Juneau Haul

A breakdown of the authentic vintage pieces hitting the physical tables in Victoria.

The Local Legend

"Egan Is Lava" Collab Tee

The ultimate Juneau insider grail. Celebrating the annual 13.1-mile brewery-to-brewery fun run collab between Devil's Club Brewing Co. and Forbidden Peak Brewery, where setting foot on Egan Drive is strictly forbidden.

The 80s Pastel

North Pole "Good Slice" Tee

A classic piece of interior Alaskana. Features a vintage Santa Claus holding a fresh pizza pie with a green circular border reading "Merry Christmas & To All A Good Slice" over a pink checkerboard background. Officially stamped 123 Elf Road, North Pole, 99705.

The Craft Beer Staple

Forbidden Peak Brewery

Authentic apparel sourced straight from the brewery hub out in Auke Bay, a staple of the modern Southeast Alaska craft beer scene. Clean minimal mustard-gold body with subdued stacked block typography.

The Cultural Grail

Sealaska Celebration 2020

Official cultural event gear from Juneau's Virtual Celebration gathering. Showcases striking Northwest Coast formline art below the powerful theme "HAVE COURAGE" on a soft sports grey heather blank.

The Weathered Slate

Juneau Drug Co. Vintage

A tribute to a downtown Front and Main Street icon serving the capital city since 1980. Features the classic yellow-gold mountain silhouette script design over a solid slate grey canvas.

The Pop Color

Parks & Rec Ski to Sea '86

An incredible multi-sport race relic. Aggressive, high-speed 1980s neon yellow brush typography pops clean off a vibrant electric teal cotton base. Forty years of Juneau history on your chest.

The History Piece

Gold Rush Days 1991

Official Juneau Gold Rush Commission apparel marking the historic 1898–1998 centennial timeline. Centers on a clean miner silhouette carrying a pickaxe over a striking red sun graphic on a pale blue base.

The Southeast Rival

Ketchikan Kings Legacy — Back

The reverse side of the Ketchikan Kings shirt: a large crowned king salmon leaping in cream ink on a deep maroon body. Pure Southeast Alaska royalty.

The Southeast Rival

Ketchikan Kings Legacy — Front

Vintage high school sports shirt carrying the iconic Ketchikan Kings legacy. Bold gold block lettering over a deep maroon cotton base — authentic Southeast regional rivalry history.

The Statement Red

JDHS Crimson Bears

Pure hometown pride sourced directly from local racks. Classic red blank holding the official Juneau-Douglas High School athletic roaring bear graphic in dark ink.

The Capital Local

Juneau Alaska Est. 1959

The ultimate local badge. Soft washed black blank with a bold centered circular seal featuring mountain peaks, a bear silhouette, and the statehood year. Worn-in and perfect.

The Outdoorsy Vibe

Camp Life

Faded forest green oversized tee with a woodcut-style illustration of a lake sunrise framed in a rectangle. Trees, water, a single dock — all the peace of an Alaskan morning captured in a single graphic.

The Youth Sports Relic

JSC 3v3 World Cup

Local youth sports tournament history. Features the classic Juneau Soccer Club mountain landscape crest with an Alaska state outline and a soccer ball locked inside a structured circular seal on a light grey blank.

The Holiday Hoops Grail

Capital City Classic

An absolute staple of the local winter sports culture. Celebrates Juneau's annual holiday high school basketball tournament with a festive Santa hat resting on a crisp vector basketball on an all-black body.

The Brewery Vintage

Alaskan Blonde Honey Ale

A vintage promo shirt from Juneau's trailblazing Alaskan Brewing Co. Showcases the classic "Ale Brewed With Honey" brown bear portrait framed in an oval crest below their iconic stylized font on a soft pale yellow blank.

The Minimalist

Glacier Swim Club Est. 1973

A nod to Juneau's competitive swim heritage. Features a clean forest green bear silhouette marching through the timberline on a heavyweight athletic yellow body. Simple, bold, unforgettable.
