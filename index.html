Why We Are Here

Cold Shirts. Warm Hearts.

We built this collection piece by piece, hunting directly through local thrift stores right in Juneau, Alaska. Every faded graphic, old-school logo, and worn hem holds a real piece of history sourced straight from the racks of a single southeast coastal town.

But these shirts didn't travel thousands of miles just to look good.

Every single dollar raised from this collection goes directly toward supporting J.R.’s medical recovery and rehabilitation journey. We're proud to bring a piece of the Far North to Victoria, Texas, rally local support, and back J.R. every step of the way.

The Curated Juneau Haul

A breakdown of the authentic vintage pieces hitting the physical tables in Victoria.

Local Grail

"Egan Is Lava" Collab Tee

The ultimate Juneau insider grail. Celebrating the annual 13.1-mile brewery-to-brewery fun run collab between Devil's Club Brewing Co. and Forbidden Peak Brewery, where setting foot on Egan Drive is strictly forbidden.

Alaskana Vintage

North Pole "Good Slice" Tee

A classic piece of interior Alaskana. Features a vintage Santa Claus holding a fresh pizza pie with a green circular border reading "Merry Christmas & To All A Good Slice" over a pink checkerboard background. Officially stamped with 123 Elf Road, North Pole, 99705.

Local Brews

Forbidden Peak Brewery

Authentic apparel sourced straight from the brewery hub out in Auke Bay, a staple of the modern Southeast Alaska craft beer scene.

Heritage Event

Sealaska Celebration 2020

Official cultural event gear from the virtual gathering in Juneau. Showcases striking Northwest Coast formline art below the powerful theme "HAVE COURAGE" on a soft sports grey heather blank.

Downtown Icon

Juneau Drug Co. Vintage

A tribute to a downtown Front and Main Street icon serving the capital city since 1980. Features the classic yellow-gold mountain silhouette script design over a solid slate grey canvas.

Classic Race

Parks & Rec Ski to Sea '86

An incredible multi-sport race relic. Aggressive, high-speed 1980s neon yellow brush typography pops clean off a vibrant electric teal cotton base.

Hometown History

Gold Rush Days 1991

Official Juneau Gold Rush Commission apparel marking the historic 1898–1998 centennial timeline. Centers on a clean miner silhouette carrying a pickaxe over a striking red sun graphic.
