Why We Are Here

Cold Shirts. Warm Hearts.

We built this collection piece by piece, hunting directly through local thrift stores right in Juneau, Alaska. Every faded graphic, old-school logo, and worn hem holds a real piece of history sourced straight from the racks of a single southeast coastal town.

But these shirts didn't travel thousands of miles just to look good.

Every single dollar raised from this collection goes directly toward supporting J.R.'s medical recovery and rehabilitation journey. We're proud to bring a piece of the Far North to Victoria, Texas, rally local support, and back J.R. every step of the way.
